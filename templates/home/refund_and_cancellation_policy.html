Refund & Cancellation Policy

This refund and cancellation policy outlines how you can cancel or seek a refund for a product or service that you have purchased through the Platform.

Cancellations

  • Cancellation requests will only be considered if made within 7 days of placing the order.
  • Cancellations may not be entertained if the order has already been processed, shipped, or is out for delivery.
  • If the product is already out for delivery, you may choose to reject it at the doorstep.
  • Cancellation is not allowed for perishable items such as flowers, eatables, etc., unless the product is proven to be of poor quality.

Damaged or Defective Products

  • Please report damaged or defective items to our customer service team within 7 days of delivery.
  • Refund/replacement requests will be processed once the seller/merchant verifies the issue.

Product Not as Expected

If you feel that the product received is not as shown on the site or not up to expectations, you must notify our customer service within 7 days of delivery. The team will review your complaint and take appropriate action.

Products with Manufacturer Warranty

For products covered under manufacturer warranty, please contact the respective manufacturer directly for resolution.

Refund Processing

In case a refund is approved by Prasanna Digital, the amount will be processed and credited to you within 7 working days.
